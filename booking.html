Frequently Asked Questions

  • What type of power outlets does Taniti use?
    Taniti uses 120-volt power outlets, the same as in the United States.
  • Is alcohol available at all hours?
    Alcohol cannot be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
  • What is the legal drinking age in Taniti?
    The drinking age is 18, although it is not strictly enforced.
  • Will I be able to communicate easily in English?
    Most younger Tanitians speak fluent English, but older residents in rural areas may not.
  • Are there medical facilities on the island?
    Yes, there is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual staff.
  • Is Taniti a safe place to visit?
    Violent crime is very rare. However, there are occasional reports of pickpocketing in tourist areas.
  • Should I be aware of local holidays when planning my visit?
    Yes. Taniti has many national holidays during which tourist attractions and restaurants may close.
  • What currency is used in Taniti?
    The U.S. dollar is the official currency, but many businesses also accept euros and yen. Most businesses also accept major credit cards.
